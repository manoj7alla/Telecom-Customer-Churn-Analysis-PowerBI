# Telecom-Customer-Churn-Analysis-PowerBI
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing telecom customer churn patterns, retention metrics, revenue at risk, and customer segmentation using SQL, DAX, and Power BI.

Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Dashboard

Project Overview -

Customer churn is one of the most critical business challenges in the telecom industry. This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies key churn drivers, and provides actionable insights to improve customer retention.
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed using SQL, DAX, and Power BI to visualize churn trends, customer segments, revenue at risk, and retention metrics.

Business Problem -

Telecom companies lose significant revenue when customers discontinue services. The objective of this project is to:
Identify customer segments with high churn risk
Analyze factors influencing customer attrition
Measure revenue impact of churn
Support data-driven retention strategies

Key Insights -
Customer Churn Rate,
Overall churn rate: 26.5%,
Contract Type Analysis,
Month-to-Month contracts contribute the highest customer churn.,
Long-term contracts exhibit significantly lower churn.,
Internet Service Analysis,
Fiber Optic customers show the highest churn rate.,
DSL customers demonstrate better retention.,
Payment Method Analysis,
Electronic Check users are more likely to churn than customers using other payment methods.,
Customer Tenure Analysis,
Most customer attrition occurs within the first 12 months.,
Longer-tenure customers are less likely to leave.,
Online Security Analysis,
Customers without Online Security services show significantly higher churn.,
